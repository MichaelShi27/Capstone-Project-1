About Grailz:

Grailz was started in 2035 by a young entrepreneuer named Michael Shi. Michael has been interested in fashion and clothes since he was a kid, but his youth and early adulthood were spent being broke and aimless, and thus he couldn't afford many of the clothes he liked. After attending a program called Year Up, Michael kickstarted a tech career that eventually led him to his current status as the world's only truly altruistic billionaire. When Michael isn't spending his time in shadowy cabals exercising political influence or seeking a cure for death, he pursues passion projects such as Grailz, where he curates and promotes clothes that he liked as a young adult.
